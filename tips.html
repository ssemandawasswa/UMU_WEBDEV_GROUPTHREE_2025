Travel Tips for Uganda Safaris

Practical advice to help you plan a smooth, respectful, and unforgettable wildlife journey.

  • Best Time to Visit: Juneâ€“August and Decemberâ€“February for gorilla trekking; consider the wet season for lush scenery but muddy trails.
  • Permits & Planning: Gorilla permits are limited; book early through an accredited operator.
  • Health & Safety: Vaccinations (as advised), anti-malarial prophylaxis, and travel insurance with medical cover.
  • Respect & Etiquette: Keep a safe distance from wildlife; follow guide instructions and local cultural norms.
  • Packing: Lightweight clothing, good walking shoes, rain gear, sun protection, and a compact first-aid kit.

What to Pack

  • Camera with extra memory and batteries; binoculars for birding.
  • Water bottle, snacks, and a small day pack for treks.
  • Insect repellent, sunglasses, and a hat for sun protection.
  • Lightweight layers for varying elevations and temperatures.
