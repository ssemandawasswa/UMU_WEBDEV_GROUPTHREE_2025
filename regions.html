Southwest: Bwindi & Mgahinga

Gorilla trekking in misty montane forests and volcanic landscapes.

North: Kidepo & Moroto

Iconic savanna scenery, dramatic skies, and remote wilderness.

Eastern: Mt. Elgon National Park

Exploring hiking, birdwatching, and exploring caves and waterfalls.

Western: Queen Elizabeth

Tree-climbing lions, crater lakes, and boat safaris on the Kazinga Channel.

Central: Lake Mburo

Wetlands, horseback safaris, and abundant wildlife in a compact footprint.

Western: Kazinga Channel

Boat cruises and the abundant wildlife, including the world's largest concentration of hippos.

Planning by Region

  • Bwindi & Mgahinga:
    Book gorilla permits early; prepare for forest trails and altitude.
  • Kidepo:
    Prepare for remote travel; connect with a local guide for park access and safety.
  • Queen Elizabeth:
    Combine game drives with the Kazinga Channel boat cruise for diverse scenery.
  • Lake Mburo:
    Great for short, accessible safaris and birdwatching close to major highways.
